CHAPTER 1

INTRODUCTION TO COMPUTING

Intro to Computing teaches fundamental computer science and information technology concepts. Students explore basics like computer systems, hardware, software, networking, and computing's societal impact. The course cultivates problem-solving skills and logical thinking, laying the foundation for advanced computing studies.

CHAPTER 2

PROFESSIONS AND CAREERS IN COMPUTING

Professions and careers in computing provide a variety of options, including jobs like a software developer, network engineer, data scientist, cybersecurity analyst, systems analyst, database administrator, and IT project manager.

CHAPTER 3 & 4

INFORMATION PROCESSING CYCLE AND COMPUTER SYSTEMS

The data processing cycle involves steps like input, processing, output, and storage. Computer systems, combining hardware and software, work together to complete tasks and follow this cycle.

CHAPTER 5

BASIC CONCEPT OF COMPUTER SECURITY

The basic concept of computer security is to protect computer systems and data from unauthorized access, harm, or theft, making sure that information stays confidential, remains intact, and is always available when needed.
